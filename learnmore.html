Our Story

In 2019, we began our business by solely renting PCs. We also sell high-quality computer accessories such as monitors, headphones, mice, and gaming chairs. That same year, we also began providing printing services. We also offer a True Money service, which includes e-load, bill payment, and cash shipment. We closed our cafe in 2020 to ensure our safety after the pandemic began. After four months, we resumed operations and began offering online deliveries.

In 2022, we organized a mini-tournament for the Special Forces participants. This mini-tournament also includes cash prizes for the top three teams. We now have a branch in Brgy. Iyam, Lucena City. In 2024, we will continue to operate and sell high-quality computer peripherals and printers, as well as printing services, true money, and sticker customization.
